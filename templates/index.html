Row-Major vs Column-Major Traversal
Row-Major — sequential
Cache efficiency—
Elements are contiguous in memory. High spatial locality — most loads share a cache line.
Column-Major — strided
Cache efficiency—
Stride = N words between accesses. Each load likely misses — thrashes the cache.
